Campus Marketplace

A place to sell or buy goods/services, exclusively for high school and college students.

About Us

There is currently no means for college students to offer their skills and services in the campus community. Campus Marketplace is an online platform where students can easily find or offer affordable services to practice their talents. Since it is exclusive to college students, the marketplace will have a positive impact on campus community, and help bring students together. the prototype.

Features

Exclusive

Only those with a tjhsst.edu email address can sign up.

Sell

Post services you'd like to sell to your peers with our easy-to-use interface.

Negotiate

Use our messaging system to chat between seller and buyer and negotiate.

Buy

Search for services or browse through available services by category. Sort options available.
